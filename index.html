How It Works

Start trading with your peers in three simple steps.

1

Sign up with your student email

We require an active .edu or university email to register. This ensures a secure, closed-community environment free from external scammers.

2

Browse or list items

Search for specific textbooks or snap a photo of that mini-fridge you no longer need. Add a title, price, and category to get started.

3

Chat and complete deals

Connect with interested buyers or sellers via chat. Agree on a price, meet up safely at the campus library or student union, and trade!
